Bitcoin Investments Website
Product Requirements Document
Platform Revival & Monetization Strategy
Executive Summary
Bitcoin Investments will be repositioned as the premier onboarding platform for new cryptocurrency investors, providing accurate, vetted information and connecting users to the best resources in the crypto ecosystem. The platform will serve as both an educational hub and a monetizable gateway, generating revenue through affiliate partnerships, advertising, premium features, and potential blockchain-based offerings.
Product Vision
Mission: Simplify cryptocurrency investment for newcomers by providing trustworthy education and connecting them with the best tools and platforms.
Target Market: Crypto-curious individuals aged 25-55 with disposable income, seeking reliable entry points into cryptocurrency investing. Secondary audience includes intermediate investors looking for advanced tools and information.
Core Features
1. Educational Hub
•	Beginner's Guide Series: Step-by-step tutorials covering blockchain basics, wallet setup, exchange selection, security best practices, and first purchase walkthrough
•	Cryptocurrency Dictionary: Searchable glossary with clear definitions, examples, and related terms
•	Video Tutorial Library: Short-form video content (3-5 minutes) covering specific topics, optimized for mobile viewing
•	Weekly Market Analysis: Easy-to-understand market reports with key trends, avoiding technical jargon
•	Risk Assessment Quiz: Interactive tool helping users understand their risk tolerance and investment readiness
2. Platform Comparison Engine
•	Exchange Comparison: Side-by-side comparison of major exchanges (Coinbase, Kraken, Binance.US, etc.) with fees, features, security ratings, and user reviews
•	Wallet Comparison: Hardware, software, and mobile wallet comparisons with security features and ease-of-use ratings
•	Tax Software Reviews: Comparison of crypto tax platforms (CoinTracker, Koinly, ZenLedger) with feature breakdowns
•	Smart Filters: Users can filter by location, experience level, investment amount, and specific cryptocurrencies supported
3. Real-Time Data Dashboard
•	Live Price Tracking: Top 50 cryptocurrencies with 24h changes, market cap, and volume
•	Fear & Greed Index: Market sentiment indicator with historical data and explanations
•	Portfolio Tracker: Free basic tracker for users to monitor their holdings (no exchange API connections required)
•	News Aggregator: Curated news from trusted sources with AI-powered summarization
4. Community Features
•	Q&A Forum: Moderated discussion boards where beginners can ask questions, with verified expert badges
•	Success Stories: User-submitted investment journeys (moderated to avoid pump-and-dump content)
•	Expert AMA Series: Monthly Ask Me Anything sessions with crypto industry professionals
•	Regional Meetup Coordination: Tools for users to organize local crypto investment groups
5. Investment Calculators & Tools
•	DCA Calculator: Dollar-cost averaging simulator showing historical performance
•	Fee Comparison Calculator: Calculate total costs across different exchanges for specific purchase amounts
•	Tax Impact Estimator: Basic capital gains calculator for US users
•	Staking Rewards Calculator: Estimate potential earnings from staking various cryptocurrencies
6. Personalization Engine
•	Onboarding Quiz: 5-minute assessment to create personalized learning path
•	Learning Progress Tracking: Track completion of guides and recommend next steps
•	Custom Alerts: Price alerts, news notifications based on user's selected cryptocurrencies
•	Recommended Resources: Dynamic content suggestions based on user behavior and skill level

5. Advanced Monetization (Pending Legal Review)
IMPORTANT: The following opportunities require thorough legal review due to securities regulations, SEC compliance requirements, and state-by-state licensing variations.
NFT Educational Platform
•	Concept: Issue NFTs as certificates of completion for educational courses
•	Revenue Model: $25-50 per NFT certificate, 2.5% royalty on secondary sales
•	Legal Considerations: NFTs may be classified as securities if they promise future returns. Focus on utility (proof of education) rather than investment potential. Consult securities attorney before launch.
Platform Token Launch
•	Concept: Utility token for premium features, community governance, reward system for contributors
•	Token Utility: Discounts on premium membership, access to exclusive content, voting rights on platform features, rewards for quality forum contributions
•	Revenue Model: Token sale (if legally permissible), transaction fees, treasury appreciation
•	Legal Considerations: Extremely high regulatory risk. SEC applies Howey Test to determine if tokens are securities. Must avoid:
○	Marketing tokens as investments
○	Promising price appreciation
○	Creating expectation of profit from others' efforts
•	Recommendation: If pursuing, engage crypto-specialized securities law firm. Consider Switzerland or jurisdiction with clear token frameworks. Budget $50K-100K for legal compliance.
Alternative: Membership Points System
•	Lower-Risk Option: Non-transferable points system (like airline miles) that avoids securities classification
•	Benefits: Earn points for contributions, redeem for premium features or merchandise. No secondary market = no securities issues.
•	Legal Risk: Minimal if points are truly non-transferable and have no cash value

Risk Assessment & Mitigation
Regulatory Risks
•	Risk: SEC classification of affiliate marketing as financial advice
•	Mitigation: Clear disclaimers that content is educational only, not financial advice. Consult with crypto-specialized attorney to review all content and affiliate relationships. Maintain registered agent address and proper business structure (LLC recommended).
Market Volatility
•	Risk: Crypto bear markets reduce interest and affiliate conversions
•	Mitigation: Diversify revenue streams (ads, subscriptions, sponsored content). Build email list aggressively to maintain communication during downturns. Create evergreen educational content that remains valuable regardless of market conditions.
Affiliate Program Changes
•	Risk: Exchanges reduce commission rates or terminate programs
•	Mitigation: Partner with 20+ affiliate programs to reduce dependency on any single source. Build direct relationships with platform partnership managers. Develop premium subscription revenue as buffer against affiliate changes.
Competition
•	Risk: Large incumbents like CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph dominate search results
•	Mitigation: Focus on beginner-specific long-tail keywords competitors ignore. Build superior calculators and tools that generate backlinks. Create highly actionable content rather than just news coverage. Emphasize community and personalization as differentiators.
SEO Algorithm Changes
•	Risk: Google updates penalize affiliate-heavy sites
•	Mitigation: Prioritize high-quality, helpful content over keyword stuffing. Build genuine community features (forum, user-generated content). Diversify traffic sources (social, email, direct) to reduce Google dependency. Follow Google's E-E-A-T guidelines religiously.
